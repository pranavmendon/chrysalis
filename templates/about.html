WHO IS LUMELume is an advanced mental health chatbot engineered to provide immediate, supportive, and accessible emotional assistance. Developed as a bridge between technology and wellness, Lume uses intuitive conversational AI to offer a non-judgmental space where users can express their thoughts and feelings in real-time. It acts as a digital companion, equipped to provide grounding techniques and insightful reflections for those navigating the complexities of daily life.





01

We took extensive precautions and ethical measures while developing Lume to ensure user safety.

03

Your privacy is a priority; all conversations are handled with high-level encryption and security.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Connect

Start a secure conversation whenever you need a non-judgmental space to talk.

2. Reflect

Lume provides grounding exercises and insights based on your shared thoughts.

3. Grow

Develop self-awareness and resilience through consistent mindful interaction.

Emergency Resources: Lume is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency services or a mental health hotline immediately.
